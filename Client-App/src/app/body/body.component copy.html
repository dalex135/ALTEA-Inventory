ALTEA

AlTEA Charity Oraganization

The purpose of this initiative is to provide educational support to poor children from underprivileged schools by donating IT Equipment’s to their Schools. The program will aim to enhance their learning by providing access to educational resources through technology. The program will be implemented through partnerships with local schools and ALTEA Association. The impact of the initiative will be measured by tracking the academic progress and success of educational standard of students.

Stories of Impact

ALTEA Organization has changed the lives of many students, teachers and authorities. The donations have changing the way they learn, percieive and quality of their learning.

ARUKGAMMANA MAHA VIDYALAYA - Kegalle District

We provided 9 Laptops, 5 Personal Computers and 1 projector to Arukgammana Maha Vidyalaya. 2017/2018, Mr Bandula Dharmathilake the Principal of Arukgammana Maha Vidyalaya mentioned that they were able to achieve the 1st place in their zone from the 15th place they were, in student academic performance due to ALTEA Association Computer donation. This is a good achievement by the School.

BOYS TOWN - Ragama

This Boys town school is managed by Dilasa Brothers. The School is an orphanage and the children don’t have their parents. They had a Computer Lab, but the computers were very old and not in working condition.
