Aus-Lanka Technological and Educational AID

The purpose of this initiative is to provide educational support to poor children from underprivileged recipients by donating IT Equipment’s to their recipients. The program will aim to enhance their learning by providing access to educational resources through technology. The program will be implemented through partnerships with local recipients and ALTEA Association. The impact of the initiative will be measured by tracking the academic progress and success of educational standard of students.

IT SUPPORT

We have organized the service of Technical Officer who will visit Schools that have received donations and inspect the condition of IT Equipment’s. He will also provide technical assistance and support related to hardware and software. The Technical Officer will help to resolve problems faced by Schools who have received donations and assist in the installation, configuration, and troubleshooting of various types of problems faced by the Schools. The main goal of IT support is to ensure the smooth operation of donated Machines, maximize the availability of technology resources, and enhance the productivity of Scholl Children of rural Schools.
